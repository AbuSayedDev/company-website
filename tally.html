Tally.ERP-9

Tally.ERP-9 is an Accounting Software by Tally Solutions Private Limited. One of the widely used accounting software in Bangladesh, ranging from Textile & Garments, Agro, Production, Construction, Services and other business areas. It is well suited for Startups, SME’s & Large Corporations. Popular for wide variety of features in Accounting, Inventory, Payroll & Audit modules. Key benefits of Tally.ERP-9 is that it is Easy to use, High user security control, Unlimited company operations with just one license.

Wezapps Limited is offering a wide range of services for the Tally.ERP-9 software in your company. We are ready to provide solutions for Tally.ERP-9. We will help you unlock and implement features that exists in the software that you need for your business.

Chart Of Accounts Design

A Chart of Accounts (COA) is an index of all the financial accounts in the general ledger of a company. In short, it is an organizational tool that provides a digestible breakdown of all the financial transactions that a company conducted during a specific accounting period, broken down into subcategories. A professionally designed Chart Of Accounts makes your financial reporting much more efficient.

We will design your chart of accounts, that is suitable for your industry. All we need is your previous CoA data for reference. Don’t have one? We will make one for you from scratch.
