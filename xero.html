Xero

Xero is a New Zealand domiciled public technology company, listed on the Australian Securities Exchange. Xero is a cloud-based accounting software platform for small and medium-sized businesses.

If you are looking for an accounting software package where you can work & monitor from anywhere anytime, Xero should be your choice. Your data is safe & secure in Xero;s server so you won’t have to care for backups or data losses. We have Xero advisors trained and ready to deliver Xero accounting software solutions implemented in your business.

Chart Of Accounts Design

A Chart of Accounts (COA) is an index of all the financial accounts in the general ledger of a company. In short, it is an organizational tool that provides a digestible breakdown of all the financial transactions that a company conducted during a specific accounting period, broken down into subcategories. A professionally designed Chart Of Accounts makes your financial reporting much more efficient.

We will design your chart of accounts, that is suitable for your industry. All we need is your previous CoA data for reference. Don’t have one? We will make one for you from scratch.

User Management and Security Control

Access control is a method of guaranteeing that users are who they say they are and that they have the appropriate access to company data. As XERO can be used by multiple employees from various departments, proper security control is must.

We take Data Security very seriously. We can setup and configure your XERO users as required and set the security control as required for the organisation.

Migration from Other software

It is a Mammoth task to migrate from your previous software to Xero. Updating backdated data entry along with daily work can be problematic & time consuming for you.

We can help you by designing, updating and reconciling the data and match it with your new conversion balance.

Receivables & Payables Management

Receivable/Payables Management is an important feature that often get ignored. Resulting bad debt & poor cash flow.
